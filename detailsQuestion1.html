The FreeStyle Skin (SKIN) can be easily replaced.

[Video subtitles]
※ This video is a video without voice


  • 1.Please read the manual carefully first and replace SKIN
    2. Use the enclosed tool to separate the main body and stand.
    3. Double-sided tape separation is required when replacing SKIN. Use the enclosed tool to separate the double-sided tape.
    ※ When removing double-sided tape or containing alcohol, solvent surfactants on the surface of the product, or do not use chemicals such as wax, benzene, and cleaning agents in the product
    4. The lens at the top faces up and slightly lifts the bottom of the SKIN to replace it.
    5. When replacing, do not apply excessive force to the lens at the top of The Freestyle and the speaker grill at the bottom. It causes damage.
    6. Be sure to remove the double-sided tape attached to the body and skin.
    7. When replacing SKIN, make sure to check the operation button and sensor position
    8. Please remove the tag on the double-sided tape attached to SKIN. If double-sided tape is not used, excitation may occur.
    9. Remove the tag at the top in the downward direction and the tag at the bottom in the upward direction.
    ※ After removing the tag, press both sides slightly to attach well
    10. Assemble the main body and stand. If it is difficult to replace, please contact the service center.
