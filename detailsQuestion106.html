How to change HDMI connection status When connecting the TV and the set-top box, you can watch the broadcast by connecting to the component and HDMI terminal. When connected to the HDMI terminal, it can be viewed with the best image quality, and when connected to the component terminal, the image quality may be lower than that of the HDMI terminal. Since basic component cables are provided when installing set-top boxes, HDMI cables must be purchased separately if you want to watch in higher definition. HDMI cable purchases can be made at nearby service centers and Internet open markets. TV/External Input of TV Remote Control Press the button. METHOD FOR RECONNECTING CABLE AND REMOVING SET-TOOP BOX If it is cloudy or unclear while watching a broadcast through a set-top box, it may be caused by a set-top box or a weak reception of a broadcast signal. In this case, you should contact the broadcaster for a set-top box or broadcast signal check [wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.]. ■ Checklist
    1.Turn off the power to the TV and set-top box (OFF), separate the cable on the back of the TV, and reconnect (HDMI, component).
    2. If it is difficult to separate from the TV, please disconnect the cable from the set-top box and reconnect (HDMI, component).
    3. Turn off the main switch on the back of the set-top box (OFF) and turn on the power again in a minute (ON).
    4. The set-top box booting process takes at least 1 to 10 minutes and is completed when the broadcast comes out normally.
