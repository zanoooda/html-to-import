STB (Settop Box) screen stops, and there are symptoms of malfunction while watching.

Expected cause It may occur due to malfunction of the set-top box device

  • Method of action TV power reset method If the phrase is not displayed on the TV screen even if you press the menu and volume button of the TV remote control, it may occur as a TV malfunction symptom. In this case, you can take simple action through TV initialization. Turn off the TV power, disconnect the power cord on the back of the TV for more than a minute, reconnect it, and use it. Disconnect the power cord on the back of the TV Reconnect the power cord on the back of the TV Reconnect the power cord of the set-top box It may occur when the set-top box stops due to a set-top box malfunction. In this case, disconnect the power cord on the back of the set-top box and reconnect it about 1 minute later before using it. The set-top box starts initial power booting and needs to be checked after normal booting about 1 to 3 minutes later. Self-diagnosis image quality test confirmation If the screen stops, you can check for abnormalities in the product using the image quality test built into the TV. [Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Image Quality Test] Press the remote control home button Move [Settings] at the bottom left Menu → Move customer support [Self Diagnosis] Selection Select [Start image quality test] Sample image stored on TV is displayed If there is no problem with the screen at the start of the image quality test, there is a possibility that it will stop or not work due to set-top box symptoms. Please contact the carrier you signed up for [Cable, Skylife, IPTV, etc.] set-top box inspection. Check HDMI cable It is recommended to use a genuine cable when connecting the TV and the set-top box with an HDMI cable. If the certified HDMI cable is not used, communication errors may cause poor image quality while watching. The genuine HDMI cable is printed or imprinted on the cable with Ultral High Speed HDMI, and if there is no corresponding phrase Please replace it with a genuine HDMI cable with an uncertified cable and use it. Check the "Ultral High Speed HDMI" print or imprint on the HDMI cable.
