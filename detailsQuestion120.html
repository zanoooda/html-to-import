Please let me know how to test the sound quality among the self-diagnosis functions of TV in 2017 and 2018.

Setting method [Click the home button of the remote control] → [Settings] → [Menu] → [Customer Support] → [Self-diagnosis] → [Sound Quality Test] [Click remote control home button] [Move left lower setting] [Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis Move] [Select to start sound quality test] [If symptoms cannot be reproduced, select No] [Select to close sound quality test completion]
※ There is a difference in remote control specifications according to the model, so you can refer to the product manual
