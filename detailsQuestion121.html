Please let me know how to test the sound quality of the 2019 TV self-diagnosis function.

Usage guide
※ There may be differences in menus according to product models

  • Please set it with the TV remote control. Setting method [Click the home button of the remote control] → [Settings] → [Menu] → [Customer Support] → [Self-diagnosis] → [Sound Test] [Click remote control home button] [Move left lower setting] [Menu → Move Customer Support ] [Self-diagnosis Selection] [Select sound test start] [If there are no symptoms of the problem, select No]
