Please let me know how to test the image quality of the 2019 TV self-diagnosis function.

Setting method - Press the home button of the remote control → Settings → Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Image quality test The menu screen varies depending on the model of the product, so please refer to the product manual together

  • [Remocon's] Home button Press] [Lower left] Set Move] [Menu → Customer support Move] [ Self-diagnosis Select] [Sample image stored on TV is displayed] [If there are no symptoms of the problem, select No] [Select to close image quality test completion]
    ※ Sample images are played on the screen during image quality tests, and if the image is clearly visible, image quality abnormalities may occur due to abnormalities in external broadcast signals, not product abnormalities. In this case, you should contact the cable broadcaster you are watching for a signal check. If the sample image is not clear during the image quality test, it may occur due to internal symptoms of LCD panel or TV. Please get an accurate diagnosis from a professional engineer for that part.
