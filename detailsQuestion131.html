Please tell me how to set the art mode.

Art mode setting method Available for How to Set The Frame Art Mode

  • Using Art Mode It is a function that displays image content such as works of art or photos as a frame without watching TV. Remote control When the button is pressed, it is switched to TV mode or art mode. In art mode If you press the button, press the button to switch to TV mode. In art mode, the brightness and color tone of the TV are automatically adjusted according to the surrounding illuminance environment, and the TV can be turned on or off automatically. (When the ambient brightness becomes dark, the screen becomes dark and is set to turn off after a certain period of time) In art mode, you can connect to the TV using the Smart Things app on your mobile device to retrieve photos from your mobile device. ■ Recent appreciation You can manage recently selected images. ■ Samsung Collection You can select images for multiple topics. (11 themes including landscape, architecture, etc.) ■ My collection You can check your favorite works or photos in Favorites, and you can select images stored on external storage devices or mobile devices and save them in your photos. ■ Art Store You can go to the art store and purchase various images. (Network required) ■ Enjoy images by time zone
    1.Use the direction button on the remote control to go to Samsung Collection or My Collection.
    2. Use the direction button on the remote control to move to one of the submenus.
    3. Use the direction button on the remote control to move to the option at the top.
    4. Press the Select button on the remote control when the work change item appears.
    5. Use the direction button on the remote control to select the time when the image will be replaced and then press the select button. ■ Import images stored in external storage
    1.Connect the external storage device where the image is stored to the TV.
    2. Use the direction button on the remote control to move to my collection and then to the connected external storage item.
    3. Use the direction button on the remote control to move to the option at the top and select Save.
    4. You can select an image by moving to the desired image using the direction button of the remote control and pressing the selection button. (Multiple selection is possible)
    5. Use the direction button on the remote control to copy to my picture at the top and press the select button.
    6. The selected image is saved in my photo. Recommended resolution: 3840 x 2160 (16:9) ■ Purchase images from art store
    1.Use the direction button on the remote control to move to the art store item.
    2. Use the direction button of the remote control to move to the desired topic and press the select button. Images that correspond to the topic appear on the screen.
    3. Use the direction button of the remote control to move to the desired image and press the select button.
    4. To purchase the selected image, use the direction button on the remote control to move to the purchase at the top and press the selection button.
    5. Select the payment method you want using the direction button on the remote control and press the Select button.
    6. Please proceed with the payment according to the instructions on the screen. ■ Join Art Store Membership
    1.Use the direction button on the remote control to go to the art store.
    2. Use the direction button on the remote control to move to membership.
    3. You can sign up for membership by entering the necessary information according to the instructions on the screen. You can use the art store without restrictions, and you can enjoy a variety of works by replacing them with new works every time they suit your taste.
