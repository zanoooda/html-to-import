Initialization method Press the home button of the remote control → Settings → Menu → General → Initialization → Password 0000 → Initialization complete after rebooting [Click the home button of the remote control] [Move left bottom setting] [General → Initialize Move] [Enter password 0000] [Select initialization] [Reboot when selecting OK and initialization completed]
