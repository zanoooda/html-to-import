Please let me know how to test the image quality among the self-diagnosis functions of the 2020 TV.

Function guide If the TV screen does not appear properly, it is possible to simply diagnose whether the cause is due to an external environment such as a product or broadcast signal and cable

  • Setting method (2020) - Remote control home button → Settings → Customer Support → Device Care → Self Diagnosis → Image quality test Press the remote control home button Move [Settings] at the bottom left Customer support → Move device care [Move] by self-diagnosis Select [Quality Test] Select Image Quality Test [Check] The test photo is displayed. Image quality test complete
    ① If the diagnosis results are normal, symptoms may occur due to broadcasting signals or abnormalities in external devices.
    ② If the problem continues, you need to check the external device or check the connection status.
    ③ If the image quality test is not normal, three additional tests are possible through [No].
    ④ If the additional test images are the same, it is necessary to check the TV and check if the software version is up to date. Image quality test [Example] selection screen Diagnosis result (explanation) Image quality test [No] selection screen Image quality test 3 times failed phrase During the image quality test, a sample image is played on the screen, and if the image is clearly visible, abnormal image quality may occur due to abnormalities in external broadcast signals, not product abnormalities. In this case, you should contact the cable broadcaster you are watching for a signal check. If the sample image is not clear during the image quality test, it may occur due to internal symptoms of LCD panel or TV. Please get an accurate diagnosis from a professional engineer for that part.
