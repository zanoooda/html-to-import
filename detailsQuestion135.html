Please let me know how to test the sound quality of the 2020 TV self-diagnosis function.

Function guide Setting method Press the home button of the remote control → Settings → Customer Support → Device Care → Self Diagnosis → Sound Quality Test Completed Press the remote control home button Move [Settings] at the bottom left Customer support → Move device care [Move] by self-diagnosis Select [Sound Quality Test] If there are no symptoms of the problem, select [Yes] During the sound quality test, the sample sound built into the TV is played, and if the sound is clearly heard, it is not an abnormality of the product Symptoms may occur due to abnormalities in external broadcast signals

  • In this case, you need to check the external device or check the connection status. If the sound quality test is not normal, it may be necessary to check whether the software version is up-to-date and the TV speaker.
