Guidance on how to install general wall hangers There is a risk of safety accidents when self-installing wall hangers, so please be extra careful about safety accidents

  • METHOD FOR INSTALLING A GENERAL WALL HOLDING Put a microfiber cloth on the floor before installing the wall hanger and put the TV on. Please fix the holdering and screw to the 4 places where the TV rear bracket is connected. Connect the magic cable / OC to the back of the TV and shape the cable. Please check the level and install a wall bracket on the wall. Please check the horizontal state of the wall bracket. Check the horizontality of the wall bracket and fix it with a screw. Pull the wall bracket forward to connect the TV. Connect the TV to the wall bracket. After placing the TV on the bracket, put the pin up to prevent safety accidents. Installation of normal wall-mounted bracket has been completed
