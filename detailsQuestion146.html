Method guide
※ Remote control pairing method varies depending on the model of the product

  • Please refer to the manual of the product you use for the remote control support model. 2013 TV Pairing Method
    1.Open the back cover of the remote control and press the "pair" button toward the TV for at least 5 seconds.
    2. The display appears in the lower left corner of the TV during connection.
    3. A complete pairing display appears in the lower left corner of the TV. 2014 TV Pairing Method
    1.Remote control's " Return + Broadcast Guide "I'm going to turn the buttons at the same time Five seconds. That's all for now.
    2. The display appears in the lower left corner of the TV during connection.
    3. A complete pairing display appears in the lower left corner of the TV. 2015 TV pairing method
    1." Return/end+additional information "I'm going to turn the buttons at the same time Five seconds. That's all for now.
    2. Bluetooth display appears in the lower left corner of the TV.
    3. A complete pairing display appears in the lower left corner of the TV. TV pairing methods in 2016, 2017, and 2018
    1.Remote control." Return + Play / Pause "I'm going to turn the buttons at the same time Five seconds. That's all for now.
    2. It shows that you are connecting to the top center of the TV.
    3. A Pairing Completion display appears in the center of the top of the TV. 2019 TV pairing method
    1.Remote control's " Return + Play / Pause "I'm going to turn the buttons at the same time Five seconds. That's all for now.
    2. Samsung Smart Remote Control shows a connecting (pairing) display.
    3. The “Completion of Pairing” sign appears.
    ※ Do not press other operation buttons until pairing completion is displayed.
    ※ If the remote control battery is exchanged or does not work after pairing the smart remote control, it may be a symptom of the remote control or TV remote control receiver. If other buttons on the remote control work but only certain buttons do not work, it can be seen as a remote control symptom, Please get an accurate judgment on the symptoms of the remote control or TV remote control receiver through our professional engineer.
