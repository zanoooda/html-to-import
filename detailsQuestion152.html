    1.Acoustic DNSe+ It is a sound field effect technology developed by Samsung Electronics that reinforces the sense of space and sound such as stereophonic sound, bass reinforcement, noise environment response, and EQ.
    2. Speaker type Down Firing + Full Range Down Firing refers to a structure in which driver units are placed facing the floor to strengthen the omnidirectional characteristics of low and ultra-low sounds and increase space control. Full Range is a speaker that plays the entire band from high to low with one speaker.
    3. Panel of Display - Crystal Black Panel General black panels and crystal black panels are types of panels, and film coatings are applied to the screen to realize clearer and more accurate contrast ratios, and in order to distinguish the degree of this coating, The name is expressed in two ways. The panels include crystal black panels, natural black panels, and black panels, and have the following characteristics. Less reflective order ☞ Crystal black panel → Natural black panel → Black panel order (black panel is least reflective) Good order of image quality (light and shade ratio) ☞ Black panel → Natural black panel → Crystal black panel order (Crystal black panel is good)
    4. What's CMR? CMR is short for Clear Motion Rate and is a notation of how clear the motion can actually be compared to 60Hz.
