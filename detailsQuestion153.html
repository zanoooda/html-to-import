I want to know how to adjust the power sound size of UN32H4200AF model.

Question content The sound effect is generated when turning off or on the TV of UN32H4200AF model

  • Please tell me how to control it. Answers In the UN32H4200AF model, the melody volume control that comes out when turning off or turning on the TV Menu → Select settings → Select melody → Select desired size → end Choose . The sleep reservation function does not work when the TV is powered off. If the volume is '0' or 'quiet' button is pressed, there is no sound when the TV is turned off.
