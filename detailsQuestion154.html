UN40H5030AF, please let me know the auto-on function.

Question content I am inquiring because the TV is not automatically turned on and the external input (HDMI) used by IPTV is not selected

  • Answers The auto-on setting function of the UN40H5030AF model can only be selected for DTV general, DTV wired, general, wired, TV channel and USB input. Other than that, HDMI, component, and external input are not possible.
