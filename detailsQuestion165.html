  • Due to the influence of external devices, the connection between the TV and the sound bar may not be smooth. Inspection method
    1.Disconnect the connection lines of all products except TV and sound bar connections.
    2. Keep the distance between the sound bar and the router at least 1m.
    3. When connecting the TV and the sound bar, it is normal, but if it occurs by connecting additional external devices such as set-tops or game consoles, please check the device. (Remove HDMI No. 13 PIN)
