  • If the voice varies from channel to channel while watching TV, it is likely to be more than a broadcast signal or a characteristic of broadcasting company (management) equipment. Broadcasting signal test method
    1.Press the Home or Menu button on the TV remote control to select Settings → Customer Support → Device Care → Self Diagnosis → Signal Information.
    2. If the antenna signal is 4 or less or the SNR value is 20 or less, it is recommended to check the broadcast signal.
    3. Signal information is a feature only available on digital broadcast channels. Automatic channel setting method
    1.Click Home or Menu on the TV remote control to select Settings → Channel / Broadcasting → Automatic Channel Settings.
    2. The number of channels searched may vary depending on the region, broadcaster, and surrounding environment.
    3. If the channel or automatic channel setting is disabled, we are currently using an external device (set-top box), so please change the external input to 'TV broadcasting'. Automatic volume control method
    1.Depending on the broadcast program produced by the broadcasting station, a voice level difference may occur, and when the channel is changed, a voice may be small or a voice may be large.
    2. It mainly occurs on wired broadcasting channels rather than regular broadcasting channels, and automatic volume function is applied to TV to compensate for different volume for each channel.
    3. When setting up the automatic volume function, the volume decreases, but all channels can be viewed at a similar volume.
    4. Click Home or Menu on the TV remote control to select and turn on the automatic volume in Settings → Sound → Expert Settings. Software update
    1.Click Home or Menu on the TV remote control to set → Customer Support → Software Update → Select Update Now.
    2. Do not turn off the TV during the update. When the update is complete, the TV turns off and on automatically. At this time, both screen and sound settings can be initialized, so it is recommended to take notes of the settings in advance.
    3. Please proceed with the update when you find the latest version.
