I can't get a voice on the 2020 TV screen.

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)

  • The TV is powered on, but the broadcast screen and voice are not available when the external device is turned off or the input source is incorrectly selected. Set-top power checking method
    1.If there is no set-top power light, please reconnect the power. When the set-top power is on standby, use the set-top remote control to turn on the power.
    2. If the power is not turned on, please contact the set-top manufacturer (communication company). Check and reconnect antenna cables
    1.If you are watching TV with an antenna, please completely separate and reconnect the antenna connection line between the TV and the wall terminal. TV power checking method
    1.Please completely separate the TV power cord and the wall power cord and reconnect them. External input switching method
    1.Press the home button on the TV remote control to select external input → set top (HDMI1).
    2. Press the home button on the TV remote control to enter externally → Select TV.
    3. Press the home button on the TV remote control to select external input → game console (HDMI4). HDMI connection terminal may vary by TV model..
