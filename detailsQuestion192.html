  • While watching TV, it got bigger and smaller on the same channel If repeated, it is likely to be more than a broadcast signal or a broadcaster (management) equipment characteristic. Broadcasting signal test method
    1.Select the home button or menu button → setting → customer support → device care → self-diagnosis → 'signal information' of the TV remote control.
    2. If the antenna signal is 4 or less or the SNR value is 20 or less, it is recommended to check the broadcast signal. Signal information is a function that can only be used on digital broadcasting channels. Automatic volume control method
    1.There may be a difference in voice level depending on the broadcast program produced by the broadcasting station, and when the channel is changed, the voice may be small or the voice may be large.
    2. It mainly occurs on wired broadcasting channels rather than regular broadcasting channels, and automatic volume functions are applied to TVs to compensate for different volume levels for each channel.
    3. When setting the automatic volume function, the volume decreases, but you can watch all channels at a similar volume.
    4. Please select the home button or menu → setting → sound → expert setting → 'automatic volume' of the TV remote control and turn it on. Software update
    1.Please select the home button or menu of the TV remote control → Settings → Customer Support → Software Update → Update now.
    2. Please proceed with the update when you find the latest version.
