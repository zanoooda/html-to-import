The TV voice is shaking in 2020, I can hear noise in the TV voice.

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)

  • If the voice is shaking or noisy, it may occur when the broadcast signal is abnormal, the external input level is too high, or does not match. Sound quality test method
    1.Select the home or menu button → setting → customer support → device care → self-diagnosis → 'sound quality test' of the TV remote control.
    2. If the reproduced sample sound is heard neatly without noise, it is a symptom of noise due to broadcast signal problems, not product problems.
    3. In this case, you should contact the broadcaster for a broadcast signal check. However, it is recommended to check the Shengwu TV that will generate noise from the played sample sound. How to take action when watching TV with set-top box
    1.Set-top box volume control can be done by pressing the +, - buttons on the set-top remote control to increase or decrease the set-top volume.
    2. If you adjust the volume with the TV remote control during normal times, please set the set-top volume to medium. Sound setting method
    1.Please select the home button or menu button → sound → expert setting → 'equalizer' of the TV remote control.
    2. If the sound is shaking in a specific sound field, please set the bass (100Hz to 200Hz) low in the expert setting.
