ATMOS Sound is not working on Netflix of 2019 TV.

Symptom → An error occurs when executing the ATMOS-enabled content of Netflix

  • ■ ATMOS? → Surround sound technology developed by Dolby Research Institute allows you to listen to ATMOS Sound with sound bar connected to TV. ■ Satisfaction conditions → Digital audio output is set to Dolby Digital + → Connect the ATMOS-enabled sound bar to HDMI ARC → Supports Dolby Digital+ with ATMOS in playback content ■ Cause of occurrence Maintain the change conditions when changing the digital output audio format. → Supports the format as the last setting condition when setting Dolby Digital or PCM. Method of action
    1.Home – Settings - Sound – Sound Output – Change to TV Speaker
    2. Home – Settings – Sound – Expert Settings – Sound Initialization Progress
    3. Home – Settings – Sound – Sound Output – Change to the device (soundbar)
    4. Home – Settings – Sound – Expert Settings – Dolby Atmos Compatible 『On』 ■ ATMOS setting method
    ① Home-Settings-Sound Selection
    ② Sound output selection
    ③ Sound output
    ④ Select to change the audio device to be played to the TV speaker
    ⑤ Select Home-Settings-Sound-Expert Settings
    ⑥ Acoustic initialization selection
    ⑦ Select an example for initializing sound settings
    ⑧ Sound setting initialization pop-up is displayed
    ⑨ Select Home-Settings-Sound-Sound Output-Change Audio Device to Play to Sound Bar
    ⑩ Home-Settings-Sound-Expert Settings-Select Dolby Atmos Compatibility (Disable → Activate)
    ※ When Dolby Atmos compatibility is activated, it is lit in blue as shown in the image below. After proceeding with the above steps in order, check the Netflix condensation to display ATMOS support.
