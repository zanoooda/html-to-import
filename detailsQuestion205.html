Please tell me how to set up Samsung Smart TV with smartphone.

Function guide How to set up Samsung Smart TV with smartphone
※ This function can only be linked to models that support smart remote control

  • There is a difference in support according to the model, so please refer to it.
    1.Please connect your Samsung account and Wi-Fi on your smartphone.
    ※ If the smartphone is connected to the island account, Samsung account information and Internet network are automatically shared with the TV.
    2. Download the SmartThings app from your smartphone and start setting up your TV.
    ※ If SmartThings app is not installed, please download and install the app from the store
    3. Please check the new TV recognized on your smartphone and press Settings.
    ※ Samsung TV can be automatically recognized on smartphone, which may vary depending on smartphone specifications. Smartphones that do not support automatic recognition can be set by manually running the SmartThings app.
    4. Please choose the place and room where the TV is installed.
    ※ ' + You can create new rooms such as bedrooms, kitchens, and family rooms by pressing the ' button.
    5. It appears as a connection progress on your smartphone.
    ※ If the smartphone is connected to the Samsung account, the Samsung account information and wireless network setting information are automatically shared with the TV.
    6. Select the Smart Hub Terms and Conditions and the Consent to Collect Personal Information button.
    ※ You can use various services and useful functions through Samsung Smart TV.
    7. Set the registered TV name and back up my TV settings data.
    ※ When initializing or replacing TV by backing up TV setting data to Samsung Cloud with Samsung account, You can restore it simply.
    8. Please check the connection status of the external device.
    ※ Please check if the external device is powered on and make sure that the external device and the one-connect are not covered by other items.
    9. You can set up artificial intelligence (AI) mode for better viewing. (Only QLED TVs in 2019 are supported and may not be provided depending on the model )
    ※ Artificial intelligence mode (AI)? Samsung TV provides a better viewing experience by detecting the surrounding environment and content being watched and automatically adjusting sound and brightness.
    10. When the location information is entered and the channel search is completed, the TV setting is completed. If you enter your area of residence, you can quickly find a service provider and set up your TV.
    ※ Now complete the TV setting easily with your smartphone.
