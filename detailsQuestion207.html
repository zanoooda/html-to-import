Function guide Samsung TV Plus TV Support Target: Smart TV model released after 2013 Samsung TV Plus provides various contents such as entertainment, movies, dramas, documentaries, and home shopping by connecting the Internet to Samsung Smart TV It is a virtual channel service that allows you to choose according to your taste and enjoy it for free

  • If you connect your smart TV wirelessly to an Internet-connected LAN line or Wi-Fi without a separate subscription process or app installation, You can use it right away. Just like choosing a terrestrial channel with a remote control, watch a video content channel numbered 500-900. How to watch Samsung TV Plus
    1.Click the Samsung TV Plus app in the bottom list of the home screen. There is no need to install a separate app, and the default app cannot be deleted.
    2. Move down or up on the broadcast guide screen to check the list of Samsung TV Plus channels. You can choose the broadcast of the channel you want.
    3. Enter number 501 to go directly to the Samsung TV Plus channel. Samsung TV Plus Channel Editing Method You can delete or restore the Samsung TV Plus channel from the channel editing screen. Samsung TV Plus Channel List Edit Channel Deleted channels do not appear in the channel list and appear gray on the channel edit screen. The restored channel reappears in the channel list. How to delete Samsung TV Plus app You can delete the Samsung TV Plus app from the bottom list of the home screen. Even if you delete the app, you can watch the Samsung TV Plus channel.
    1.Move focus from the bottom list of the home screen to the Samsung TV Plus app.
    2. Press the down button. Disable > Deletion Click on.
