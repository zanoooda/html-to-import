I think the wall-mounted installation is tilted.

Expected cause Wall hangers may be tilted, or they may appear to be tilted by optical illusions due to wall names, art walls, etc

  • When the wall hanger is tilted Optical illusion due to wall art wall, etc. Method of action When installing wall hangings, the horizontal system is used to level them, so the symptoms of tilting to one side may appear to be tilted horizontally due to tolerances in use or to optical illusions due to wall art walls. Horizontal adjustment screws can be adjusted on the rear wall hanger to adjust the horizontal, but there may be a risk of safety accidents such as TV crashes during the adjustment process, and in this case, an engineer should visit and check accurately. If you do horizontal adjustment yourself, please pay special attention to safety accidents. [Horizontal Adjustment Method – General Wall Hanging] The rear wall mount of the TV can be leveled by adjusting the height adjustment screw at both ends.
