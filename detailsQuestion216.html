  • The corners of the TV screen look dark or bright. The TV screen looks stained.
    2. Watching a monochromatic screen in an environment where the TV is too dark may show image quality characteristics according to the structure of the TV panel.
    3. Screen test method
    4. Click Home or Menu on the TV remote control to select Settings → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Start Screen Test.
    5. Please “check” the image quality test notification message. Sample images for testing are displayed.
    6. It is normal if the screen stain is not visually identified 1M ahead of the TV in a bright environment around the TV.
