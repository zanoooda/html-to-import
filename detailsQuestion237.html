METHOD FOR USING VOICE RECOGNITION OF UHD 9000 MODEL You can use certain menus and functions of your TV more easily using your voice

  • To accurately recognize a user's voice, you must pronounce it slowly and accurately from the language in the System Speech Recognition menu to the selected language. Voice recognition includes interactive voice recognition that is used as if talking to a TV and command-type voice recognition that is used only with a set command. To use interactive voice recognition, you must have completed Internet connectivity, smart hub settings, agreement on terms and conditions, and time settings. When voice recognition is executed without setting, a setting guide pop-up is displayed. If you complete the settings according to the instructions, you can use interactive voice recognition. If you don't set it up, you can use command-type voice recognition. In this case, only "defined commands" are available. Before using voice recognition, read and familiarize yourself with precautions, and then use voice recognition.
    1.Complete the settings for user and TV voice in voice recognition (Menu > System > Voice Recognition).
    2. Press the voice recognition button of Samsung Smart Control (Smart Remote Control) and tell the command to the appropriate size for the microphone.
    ① Learning how to use basic voice recognition Focus shift If you say the command you want, "left," "right," "up," or "down," the focus moves. However, it can only be used in situations where focus can be moved. Item selection When you say "Select," select the item where the focus is located. Viewing Full Voice Commands If you say "Help," you can check the entire voice command by category. Tell me the category you want. Turn off voice recognition Say "End voice recognition," or press the voice recognition button. End voice recognition. Only the specified voice command can be recognized, and depending on the version, the voice command may be different from the command written in the manual.
    ② Using voice recognition as if talking to a TV You can direct various actions such as viewing reservation and recording reservation, VOD and app search and execution, without having to learn specific commands or remember keywords. For example, , "*** (title) Record reservation. If you say, ", the TV sets up a reservation recording for the program. TV voice on/off When you talk to a TV with voice recognition, you can turn on or off the voice from the TV. System > Speech Recognition From the menu. TV voice Choose . Listening to TV voice with desired gender voice System > Speech Recognition From the menu. Phonetic-gender After selecting , select the desired voice gender. You can hear the TV voice as the voice of the selected gender.
