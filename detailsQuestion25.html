How to solve 99% (98%) of additional TV devices, or 15-100, 200, 300 error codes

  • Solution
    ① Use your smartphone at the location where the product is installed Check the propagation sensitivity between the product and the Wi-Fi router (
    ※ If the antenna is less than 3 spaces, please check the location of the router or the wireless communication environment through the carrier. )
    ② Turn off the TV device, turn it on again, and try to reconnect the TV device ( If unresolved, proceed with 3 additional times ) If you fail to register on the SmartThings cloud server due to a product signal abnormality, please turn off the product, turn it back on, and reconnect it.
    ③ If the name of the Wi-Fi router is Korean, an error may occur. Change to English Please try again later.
