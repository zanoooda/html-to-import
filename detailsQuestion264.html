    1.Press the home or menu button of the TV remote control to select the input you want to switch from the external input. METHOD FOR SWITCHING FROM TV BROADCAST SCREEN TO SET-TOOP

    1.Press the Home or Menu button on the TV remote control to select “External Input” or “TV.” METHOD FOR SWITCHING FROM SETTOP BROADCAST SCREEN TO GAME SCREEN
