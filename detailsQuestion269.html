  • The TV terminal doesn't work well. It may occur if the “antenna cable”, “AOC cable”, and “HDMI cable” are molded or reinserted without time after installing the TV. Please take enough time to connect the cable. Correct cable shaping method
    1.HDMI cables have different up/down shapes, so please check and insert them. Excessive insertion causes damage. USB correct insertion method
    1.In the case of USB, the appearance is the same, so please check the inside and insert it so that it can be engaged.
    2. Excessive insertion causes damage. AOC Cable Correct Insertion Method
    1.The AOC cable has a different shape up and down, so please check it and insert it.
    2. Excessive insertion causes damage.
