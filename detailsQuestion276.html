  • I can't get a voice on a specific TV channel. If the voice varies from channel to channel while watching TV, it is likely to be more than a broadcast signal or a characteristic of broadcaster equipment. Broadcasting signal test method
    1.Click Home or Menu on the TV remote control to select “Settings,” “Customer Support,” and “Start Signal Information.”
    2. If the antenna signal is 4 or less or the SNR value is 20 or less, it is recommended to check the broadcast signal.
    3. Signaling information is only available on digital broadcast channels. Check and reconnect antenna cables
    1.If you are watching TV with an antenna, please completely separate and reconnect the antenna connection line between the TV and the wall terminal.
    2. Please reconnect One Connect and TV.
    3. Please separate and assemble the AOC cable from the TV and One Connect. Automatic channel control method
    1.On the TV remote control, click Home or Menu to select “Settings,” “Channel/Broadcast,” and “Auto Channel Settings.”
    2. The number of channels searched may vary depending on the region, broadcaster, and surrounding environment.
    3. If the channel or automatic channel setting is disabled, it is an external device, not a public wave signal, so contact the set-top and carrier to receive guidance on automatic channel setting. Software update
    1.Click Home or Menu on the TV remote control to select Update now in “Settings,” “Customer Support,” and “Software Update.”
    2. Do not turn off the TV during the update.
    3. When the update is complete, the TV turns off and on automatically.
    4. At this time, both screen and sound settings can be initialized, so it is recommended to take notes of the settings in advance.
    5. Please proceed with the update when you find the latest version.
