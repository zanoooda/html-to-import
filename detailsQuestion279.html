  • I see a dot on the TV screen. (Based on defective pixels)
    1.In the case of digital broadcasting, the camera being filmed looks like a defective pixel on TV due to foreign matter or dust When shooting in the dark, bright light spots may shine in several places.
    2. At this time, if there are no symptoms when watching other broadcasts by switching to the old channel, it can be seen as a problem according to the broadcaster's shooting signal.
    3.. If the same part occurs when switching other channels or watching other broadcasts, inspection is required. Screen test method
    1.Click Home or Menu on the TV remote control to select “Settings,” “Customer Support,” “Self Diagnosis,” and “Start Screen Test.”
    2. Please “check” the image quality test notification message. Sample images for testing are displayed.
    3. “Do you still see the symptoms of the problem in the test video? The message ” appears.
    4. Select “No” when the image quality test screen appears normally.
    5. Please check the broadcast signal rather than the TV problem.
    6. If you have the same symptoms in the additional sample image, you'd better check the TV. Defective pixel checking method
    1.Symptoms that appear purple in black dots or white patterns on the screen allow one defect per million, For FHD TVs, up to 6 are normal.
    2. The point on the screen is bright, and if the same phenomenon occurs on all channels, inspection is required.
