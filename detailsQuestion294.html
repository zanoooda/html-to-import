TV Plus service is not available.

Contents of inquiry Since a few days ago, Internet services such as YouTube have been normal, but only TV Plus services have not been available

  • What's the reason? Answers The TV PLUS service is a broadcast that watches high-definition content through a network. If TV PLUS does not work, please use it after working as below.
    1.Menu → Smart Hub → Please use it again after initializing Smart Hub. After initialization, you must agree to the terms and conditions of the smart hub.
    2. Turn off the TV, unplug the power cord, and plug it back in after 1 to 2 minutes.
    3. Please wait up to 5 minutes with the power on and try using TV PLUS again.
    4. Please check the TV PLUS channel by uploading the channel in remote control external input → TV mode (TV PLUS).
