Want to know how to deal with the safe mode of TV.

How to take action when safe mode appears Please completely remove the power cord connected to the set-top box and reconnect it in 5 minutes

  • Detailed Symptoms: The phrase "Safe Mode" appears at the bottom left of the screen while watching TV.
    ※ 'Safe mode' display occurs in an environment using a set-top box, and occurs when the set-top box is not booted normally or enters the mode. ☞ TV product function does not display 'safe mode'. - If the symptoms are the same, please contact the set-top box company you signed up for (the carrier you signed up for).
