    1.Don't shock the product. - The screen display may be damaged and may cause electric shock / fire.
    2. Do not place heavy objects, toys, etc. on top of the product. - If you hang on to the product, the product may fall or the product may fall and get hurt.
    3. Do not spray water or detergent directly on the product. - The appearance of the product may be damaged or the mark may be erased. - It can cause electric shock / fire.
    4. Insert the battery according to the polarity (+,-) - If the polarity is not correct, the battery may rupture or the internal liquid may leak, contaminating, damaging, or causing fire, and may cause injury.
