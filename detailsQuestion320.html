Intermittent abnormalities occur when updating TV firmware.

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)

  • Please confirm before receiving the service. (TV firmware update method, intermittent abnormality occurs)
    1.If you want to keep it in the best condition when using TV, or if you have abnormal symptoms intermittently, please update it to the latest version.
    2. Home → Settings → Customer Support → Soft Update → Now Update → When you find the latest version, please enter the update and proceed with the update.
    3. Please be careful not to turn off the TV during the update.
    4. When the update is complete, the TV turns off and on automatically. At this time, both screen and sound settings can be initialized, so please take notes of the settings in advance.
    5. If the symptoms are the same after the update, please disconnect and reconnect the power cord of the TV once again.
