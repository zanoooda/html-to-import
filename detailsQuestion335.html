Self-diagnosis execution confirmation This symptom may occur due to a broadcast signal or TV abnormality, and the sound quality test function can check whether the product is abnormal or not. [Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Sound Quality Test] Self-diagnosis execution confirmation Press the remote control home button Move [Settings] at the bottom left Menu → Customer support → Move self-diagnosis Select [Start sound quality test] During the sound quality test, the sample sound built into the TV is played, and if the sample sound played is heard neatly without noise, it is a symptom of noise due to broadcasting signal problems, not product problems. In this case, please contact the broadcaster to check the broadcast signal [wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.]. If noise is generated in the reproduced sample sound, it may occur due to abnormalities in the speaker or voice circuit and must be checked.
