Smart remote control does not work.

Expected cause It may be caused by a lack of remaining battery capacity of the remote control or a lack of pairing connection

  • Method of action Remote control battery reinsertion It may be caused by remote control malfunction. OPEN the rear battery cover to remove the battery and insert it again after 1 minute. Check remote control pairing It can be used only when the remote control battery is reinserted or paired when the battery is replaced. When replacing the battery, please replace it with an alkaline battery and use it. When using some manganese batteries, it is not recommended to use them because there may be symptoms of intermittent remote control failure due to insufficient battery voltage. Pairing settings must be reset after battery replacement.
