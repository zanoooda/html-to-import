Please let me know how to test the image quality of the 2016 TV self-diagnosis function.

Setting method [Click the home button of the remote control] → [Settings] → [Customer Support] → [Self-diagnosis] → [Quality Test] However, if it is not a smart remote control, please press the menu button

  • [Click remote control home button] [Click Settings on Screen] [Customer Support Press] [Self-diagnosis Press] [Click Image Quality Test] [Click the OK button]
    ※ Sample images are played on the screen during image quality tests, and if the image is clearly visible, image quality abnormalities may occur due to abnormalities in external broadcast signals, not product abnormalities. In this case, you should contact the cable broadcaster you are watching for a signal check. If the sample image is not clear during the image quality test, it may occur due to internal symptoms of LCD panel or TV. Please get an accurate diagnosis from a professional engineer for that part.
