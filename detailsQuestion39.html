How to use TV PC on TV in 2021 (Connect PC with Easy Connection)

Connecting PC with Easy Connection Use the PC on TV - Easy Connection app to remotely use your PC

  • You can automatically set up a remote PC and connect it at once.
    1.First, download the Easy Connection app from smsng.co/PConTV through the web browser and install it on your PC.
    2. Run the PC on TV - EasyConnection app to log in with the same Samsung account as the TV.
    3. If the PC is powered on and connected to the network, the PC is automatically displayed on the PC on TV home screen.
    4. My device. You can find devices connected to the TV in . If you select a device name, you can connect directly to your PC.
    ※ The detailed address of smsng.co / PConTV is http://smsng.co/PConTV.
    ※ Terms of use; : TV and laptop are the same Log in with your Samsung account In the world today. “My device” can be viewed when selecting “External input” → “PC on TV” by pressing the home button of the TV remote control. Press the home button of the remote control
    ※ Depending on the user network environment such as firewall setting, router type, and wireless signal strength, problems such as speed degradation, disconnection, and connection restriction may occur.
