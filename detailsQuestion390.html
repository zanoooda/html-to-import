It's slow when switching channels.

Expected cause It may occur due to set-top box malfunction or TV malfunction

  • Method of action When watching a TV broadcast by connecting a set-top box, the TV acts as a monitor and is changed to the channel you want to watch in the set-top box, so the channel cannot be changed. It may occur due to set-top box malfunction symptoms. Remove the power cord of the set-top box for about a minute and reconnect it. In the same case, contact the broadcaster (cable / skylife / IPTV) for an inspection of the set-top box. Initialization method If the input mode is automatically changed, it may occur as a TV malfunction symptom, and in this case, it must be initialized under factory shipment conditions. After [Caution] initialization, you must proceed with the initial setup. ■ Model before 2010 Initialization function is not applied. → Remove the power cord for about 1 minute and reconnect. ■ 2010 ~ 2011 model Press the [End] button in the middle of the remote control for 10 seconds. In the center of the TV screen, "Revert all settings to their initial state. "A message is displayed and select OK to proceed with initialization. Remote initialization guide Even if the menu settings are initialized, if they are the same, they must be initialized under factory shipment conditions. Initialization of factory shipment conditions can only be carried out through remote consultation.
