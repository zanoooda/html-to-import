I wonder how to connect to TV SmartThings (Digital Hub) in 2021. (IR remote control device)

IR remote control device connection method It automatically detects home appliances that use general IR remote controls around TVs such as air conditioners, fans, and air purifiers and connects them to TVs


  • 1.When the IR remote control of a general home appliance is operated around the TV, it can be automatically detected on the TV.
    2. Select the 'Register' button from the notification pop-up on the TV screen
    3. To operate the device on the TV, you need to test the remote control. Select the power button on the screen and turn on the device.
    4. Connected devices can be controlled by TV remote control or voice.
    5. Press the home button on the TV remote control SmartThings → Select registered device → Select device operation to control
    ※ This function may not be supported depending on the model.
    ※ Screen image may vary by model. SmartThings is a digital hub. If the remote does not work, change the battery of the remote control to a new battery and check the operation status.
    ※ If it is not solved by the above method, it may occur due to TV IR sensor or remote control IR symptoms. Please get an accurate diagnosis from a professional engineer for that part.
