Please tell me how to test the image quality of the 2013 TV self-diagnosis function.

Image quality test method If there is a problem with the screen, you can check if the TV image is being output normally

  • [Virtual remote control setting method] Press the [Virtual Remote Control] button on the remote control → [Menu] → [Customer Support] → [Self Diagnosis] → [Quality Test] Press the virtual remote control button Press the menu button on the screen Click Customer Support Self-diagnosis press Picture quality test press If you press OK, the test video will come out. [IR remote control setting method] IR remote control receiving infrared light Press the [Menu] button on the remote control → [Customer Support] → [Self Diagnosis] → [Quality Test] Press the menu button [Press] using the direction key Click Customer Support Self-diagnosis press Picture quality test press If you press OK, the test video will come out.
    ※ Sample images are played on the screen during image quality tests, and if the image is clearly visible, image quality abnormalities may occur due to abnormalities in external broadcast signals, not product abnormalities. In this case, you should contact the cable broadcaster you are watching for a signal check. If the sample image is not clear during the image quality test, it may occur due to internal symptoms of LCD panel or TV. Please get an accurate diagnosis from a professional engineer for that part.
