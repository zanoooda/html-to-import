When I'm watching TV or turning it off or on, it makes a thud.

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)


  • 1.When you turn the product on and off to watch TV, you can make various sounds such as "Ttaktak," "Ttuk," and "Ttuk."
    2. This can be caused by shrinkage/expansion of plastic parts according to changes in the temperature of the product or ambient temperature.
    ※ Screw fastening part, stand, chassis connection part, etc. of the product
    3. This phenomenon is common in products with a lot of heat changes, and it is similar to the sound of a thud as the external temperature drops sharply when the car is turned off in winter, and the sound of a "thud" when water enters a cup containing ice.
    4. If indoor/outdoor temperature changes such as "spring, fall, and winter" are severe during the four seasons, the frequency of occurrence may increase. Especially during quiet or late-night hours, you can feel it louder.
    5. It usually occurs in newly purchased products and the sound can decrease over time. It's not a product problem, so you can use it with confidence.
    6. If there is a lot of noise periodically, please contact Samsung Electronics Service for an inspection.
    ※ Not intermittent symptoms If you can hear the thud continuously more than 10 times inside the TV, you can expect it to be a TV circuit board symptom. Please get an accurate diagnosis from a professional engineer for that part.
