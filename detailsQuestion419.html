How to assemble the TV stand.

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)

  • Assembling a stand
    1.Let's find out how to assemble the stand when reinstalling the TV after purchasing it for the first time or moving.
    2. Please check the components before assembling the stand.
    3. The components are 1 stand, 1 stand guide, and 8 assembly screws.
    ※ Components may vary depending on the model, so please refer to the manual.
    4. First, assemble the stand and stand guide. Place the stand guide on the stand. Please adjust the screw hole at this time.
    5. Use the driver to fasten two screws each on the top and back of the stand guide.
    ※ When using an electric driver, adjust the screw line so that it does not wear out.
    6. Assemble the assembled stand and TV body.
    7. Place a soft cloth on the table and lie down so that the TV screen faces to the floor.
    8. Put the assembled stand into the hole at the bottom of the TV. At this time, push the stand to the end and assemble it.
    9. Please fasten the screws after the assembly is completed.
    ※ When using an electric driver, adjust the screw line so that it does not wear out.
    10. When screwing, please fasten the bottom first and then fasten the top.
    11. After the assembly is completed, please stop the TV and check if the left and right sides are tilted. If there is a left and right slope, please reassemble the stand guide.
