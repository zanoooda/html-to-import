I can see a rainbow-colored band.

Expected cause It can be caused by the signal inspection of the broadcasting company

  • Method of action Broadcasting signal check guide The symptom of a rainbow-colored band on a specific broadcast channel is not a product abnormality, but a symptom caused by a broadcast signal. You should contact the broadcaster (cable, IPTV) you are watching and get confirmation. Self-diagnosis method You can check the abnormality/nothingness of the product through the image quality test function applied to the product. How to check image quality test Select [Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Image Quality Test]. Look at the test screen and check for abnormalities. Menu - Customer Support Self-diagnosis Image quality test During the image quality test, the high-definition image built into the TV is displayed on the screen. If the rainbow-colored band does not overlap and the TEST image is clearly visible, , It is not a product problem, but a symptom caused by broadcasting companies (cables, IPTVs) sending the above pattern of screens when checking broadcasting equipment or sending test broadcasts on their own. If it continues to occur for a long time, you should contact the broadcaster (cable, IPTV) for confirmation.
