(Apply clear zoom broadcasting method) What's a clear zoom? Quadrature Amplitude Modulation (QAM) is a technology mainly used for high-speed digital modulation to improve transmission efficiency in limited frequency bands. It is a method of modulating by combining the amplitude and phase of the carrier wave. In the case of digital cable broadcasting, QAM is used as a channel transmission method, and "Clear QAM" refers to a device that can receive channels other than unencrypted channels (paid channels).
