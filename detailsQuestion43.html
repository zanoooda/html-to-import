Connect PC with TV Samsung Dex in 2021

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)


  • ※ You can connect Samsung DeX wirelessly on TV. This feature is only available on mobile devices that support wireless Samsung DeX.
    ※ Mobile devices that support Samsung DeX should be checked at Samsungdex.com. ■ Connect to Samsung Dex
    1.Pull the notification window of the mobile device down from the top of the screen and move to the left to select "Dex"
    2. From the searched device list, select the TV you want to connect to and click Start Now.
    3. In the notification window displayed on the TV screen, press the "Allow" button to accept the connection request.
    4. Complete the connection according to the instructions on the screen. When the connection is completed, the Samsung DeX screen appears on the connected TV.
    5. Mobile devices connected by Dex are automatically displayed on the PC on TV home screen. You can find devices connected to the TV on “My Device”
    6. If you select a device name, you can connect directly to your mobile device.
