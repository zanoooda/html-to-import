The screen looks stained and bruised.

Expected cause
1

  • If you press the panel or hit it slightly with your hand or other object, it may appear temporarily.
    2. It may occur without any contact due to panel defect. If there is a stain or bruise on the panel without impact or contact, there may be a panel abnormality. In this case, engineer check is required. Method of action How to check There is a backlight (light source) on the back of the panel. It can occur when the panel is impacted with a hand or other object, which is not an abnormality of the product. Self-diagnosis confirmation method You can simply check for abnormalities in the panel through the self-diagnosis function. The entry path is [Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Image Quality Test], and if the same stain occurs on the image quality test screen, engineer inspection is required. ■ How to enter image quality test - TV setting → customer support → self-diagnosis → image quality test - TV setting → customer support → device care → video test, or image quality test
