I see sunspots/rockspots/whitespots/marks. (defective pixels)

Expected cause - Due to technical limitations in the manufacturing process of TV panel manufacturers, pixels of about 1 PPM (1,000,000:1) may appear black (Dark Dot), but this does not affect the performance of the product

  • Method of action Broadcast signal check guide In the case of recently transmitted digital broadcasting, it is the case of filming in a dark place or appearing to be a TV defective pixel due to foreign matter or dust on the camera being filmed. Bright spots can shine in many places. At this time, if there are no symptoms when switching to another channel or watching another broadcast, it can be seen as a problem according to the broadcaster's shooting signal. If points occur in the same part when switching other channels or watching other broadcasts, inspection is required, and panel abnormalities can be diagnosed in the settings function on the TV. Self-diagnosis method - TV setting → customer support → self-diagnosis → image quality test - TV setting → customer support → device care → video test, or image quality test
    ※ The menu selection order varies depending on the TV model.
    1.Remote control Home button Please press .
    2. Set Please choose.
    3. Customer support Please move to.
    4. Device care Please move to.
    5. Self-diagnosis Please go to and choose.
    6. Image quality test Please check the screen status by selection. In cases of abnormality; Bright Dot: It's ideal to have at least one pixel that looks red, green, blue, purple, sky, yellow, and white in a black pattern. If it is normal Dark Dot: With pixels that look black in certain patterns, TV panel resolution can be seen up to 1 per 1,000,000. How to check Dark Dot by pattern - R.G.B pattern : Pixels that look black in each pattern - White pattern : Red, green, blue, purple, sky, yellow, black-looking pixels
    ※ Fine foreign matter inside the panel is not included in pixel defect.
