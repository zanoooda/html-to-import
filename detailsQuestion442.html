Question content We use a set-top box as a UN40F6330AF product

  • Not long ago, when I turn on the TV after setting up the smart hub, it keeps turning on with the smart hub window. How can I watch it on the TV screen right away? Answers Press the power of the UN40F6330AF product and take the following measures to appear on the TV broadcast screen immediately when the set-top box is turned on.
    1.[Virtual remote control] of TV remote control ( ) Press the button.
    2. After selecting the menu, select 'Smart Function'.
    3. After selecting ON TV, you can change the [Auto Start] function to 'Disable'.
