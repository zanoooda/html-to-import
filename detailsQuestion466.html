The voice recognition TV won't turn off.

Expected cause
1

  • If you turn off the power immediately without turning off the power after floating the help bar on High TV while watching TV, the power will not be turned off by voice recognition.
    2. It may occur if the voice recognition function is not turned on.
    3. If the surrounding noise or voice is small, it may not work. Method of action Speech recognition utterance The way to turn off the power through voice recognition is to fire with a high TV (smart TV) and turn off the power when the lower help bar is floating. Please check again how to turn off the power and use it. Voice recognition utterance word when watching TV
    1.If you command “High TV” while watching TV, the above help bar appears at the bottom of the screen.
    2. Turn off the power - When you say “Exit”, the power off function works. Check voice recognition settings You need to check the voice recognition settings to use the voice recognition function. Please change the voice recognition function to On and use it. Menu → Select "System" System → Select "Voice and Motion Recognition" Voice and Motion Recognition → Select "Voice Recognition" Voice recognition → Select "on" Voice recognition sensitivity check If the utterance is not recognized well while using voice recognition, you should check the voice recognition sensitivity setting. If your voice is small or there is ambient noise, please change the voice recognition sensitivity setting to a higher level and use it. System → Voice and Motion Recognition → Select "Voice Recognition" Voice recognition → Select "voice sensitivity" Voice sensitivity → Set high in "ring" Voice sensitivity → Set "recognition sensitivity" to high Confirmation of surrounding environmental conditions If the surrounding noise is severe or the utterance (voice) is small, it may not work. Please check the distance conditions within 1 to 4 m of the TV standard while the surrounding noise is low and use the voice recognition function again.
