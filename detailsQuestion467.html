  • Method of action Remote control battery replacement description Most of the symptoms that the remote control does not work are caused by the lack of remaining battery capacity of the remote control. The battery of the remote control provided at the time of purchase is naturally discharged in the distribution process, so it is difficult to use it as much as expected, and the remote control battery must be replaced with a new one. In the case of products sold as cheap bundled products, the usage time is much shorter than that of genuine batteries, so it is recommended to use genuine batteries packaged in pairs. Battery polarity confirmation explanation When replacing the remote control battery, the battery polarity may be wrong and the remote control may not work. You need to connect the battery - pole to an area that protrudes like a battery insertion spring. If the battery + pole is connected to a spring-like protruding area, the remote control does not work. Unlike the old remote control in the past, the remote control of the recently sold model is equipped with smart functions and has high power consumption, so even batteries that used to operate normally in other products It may not work normally on TV. When replacing the remote control battery, you must replace the pair together.
