  • ※ Eco-friendly smart remote control can be used only by charging the battery without changing the disposable battery. ■ How to check the remaining amount of remote control battery
    1.Press the Home or Menu button on the TV remote control to set → General → Power and Power Saving → Check the remaining amount of remote control battery.
    2. However, depending on the frequency of remote control use, the usage time after battery charging may vary. * The remote control provided may vary depending on the model you purchased, and this feature is only supported for models using eco-friendly smart remote control. ■ TV remote control charging method
    1.There is a solar cell on the back of the TV remote control that can charge the remote control.
    2. When the remote control is not in use, the back of the remote control can be placed upward to charge through natural and indoor light.
    3. If the remote control battery is insufficient and does not work, connect the USB cable to the bottom of the remote control and use it after charging.
    4. When charging through a solar cell, be careful not to have the remote control between sofas or under the table.
