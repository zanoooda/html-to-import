What is MHL?

MHL function guide What is MHL? It is a function that connects the micro USB terminal of the mobile device and the HDMI terminal of the TV to show the screen of the mobile device as it is on the screen of the TV

  • In addition, mobile phones can be charged through TV, and mobile phones can be controlled through TV remote control. How can I see MHL? You must have a mobile device and TV that supports MHL, and you can watch it by connecting an MHL-only cable or an MHL dongle (HDTV adapter). Does Samsung TV support all ports? When using MHL-only cable, MHL is supported only for HDMI
    3. What kind of TV model can be supported? It is a model of LED 7 series or higher and PDP 8 series in 11 years. What kind of mobile device model can be supported? Compatibility verification has been completed for the Galaxy S2 model, and for other MHL-supported mobile devices, please contact the device manufacturer. I am the buyer of the existing 11-year TV 7000. How can I do MHL? If you download the latest SW from the Samsung website and upgrade it, you can use MHL. How do I connect to MHL? 1) When using MHL-only cable: See Figure 1 on the right - One side of the MHL cable is connected to the micro USB terminal of the mobile device, and the other side is connected to the HDMI3 of the TV. 2) When using MHL dongle (HDTV adapter): See Figure 2 on the right - After connecting the power of the MHL dongle, one side of the MHL dongle connects to the micro USB terminal of the mobile device and the HDMI terminal of the TV with the HDMI cable. What is the resolution you support? MHL can be supported up to 1920X1080 at 30Hz. Can't I turn on the TV with my cell phone like Anynet+? MHL does not support turning on TV with mobile device. Compared with the cell phone, the picture quality is lower. If the resolution of the one source is low, the TV screen may look worse than a mobile phone because of its large size. Or, if the image resolution sent from the mobile phone is low, the image quality may look bad.
