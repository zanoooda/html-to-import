When I log in to the smart hub, the phrase "check the password" appears and I can't log in.

The reason why the password confirmation phrase appears The ID is the same in the Smart Hub account and the Samsung Apps account, but the password is different, but it is caused by the user entering another password without recognizing it

  • Solution
    1.If you remember two account passwords [Login Screen] Enter Samsung Apps site account and password [Check Password] Enter Smart Hub account and password
    2. Cannot remember two account passwords
    ① Users issue temporary passwords through 'Reset Password'
    ② After checking the temporary password through e-mail confirmation, enter the password of Samsung Apps when logging in to Smart Hub
    ③ Enter a temporary password issued from password verification
    ④ Integrated completion
    ※ The integrated password is not a temporary password, but a password entered when logging in to the smart hub
