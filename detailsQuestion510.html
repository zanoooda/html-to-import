I'd like to know how to install the LED TV stand. (Applicable model: UN32EH4000F)

Stand installation method Assemble the stand before removing the TV from the packaging

  • When installing the stand, use only the components and parts provided. caution When assembling, separate the front and back sides of each component. At least two people carry the TV together. Stand up the product and tighten the screws. If you lay down the TV and tighten the screw, it can tilt to one side.
