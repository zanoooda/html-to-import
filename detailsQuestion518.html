The volume suddenly increases or decreases.

Automatic volume setting status guide ■ Cause The volume size is wrong because the volume size of each broadcasting station channel is transmitted differently ■ Target model LED-TV, LCD-TV, PDP-TV ■ Automatic volume setting function Off: Turn off the automatic volume function

  • Normal: Automatically adjust the volume when changing to another channel. (Recommendation) Late Night: Automatically adjusted to low volume. Late-night mode is effective when watching at night by lowering the volume. ■ How to operate automatic volume setting function Select [Normal] from Menu → Sound → Speaker Settings → Automatic Volume → [Off].
