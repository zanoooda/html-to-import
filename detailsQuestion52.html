Usage guide Using Bixby: You can control the TV by voice by telling the command to the microphone on the Samsung Smart Remote Control


  • 1.To use Bixby, the TV must be connected to the Internet.
    2. To use Bixby, you need to register and log in to your Samsung account. If you press the button, say the desired command, and then take your hand off the button, you can recognize what the user said on the TV and execute the function. Once you press the button, the Find Now button appears at the bottom of the screen. Press the Select Remote Control button on the Find Now item to go to the Bixby usage screen. ▶ Example of using Bixby utterance - TV control: Turn off the power , go back, shut down the whole thing, select the green button. Turn up the volume, turn down the volume, mute it. Show me HDMI number one. Change the volume to 20. What's the volume? ▶ Know Before Using Bixby - Speech recognition language and functions may vary depending on the region. - If a language different from the national language is set, some support features are limited. - The Bixby function is available only when the TV is connected to the Internet. - Even if it is connected to Internet, there may be no response due to Bixby server error. - If Bixby is updated, the existing function operation specifications may be changed. - Bixby function is only available on Samsung smart remote control with built-in microphone and TV with voice recognition function. ▶ Long-distance voice support (using microphone inside remote control) You can start a conversation with Bixby right away by saying "High Bixby" to start by calling Bixby by voice without pressing the remote control voice button. After saying "High Bixby," the Bixby icon is activated at the bottom of the TV screen and the tone rings. Tell me what you want. (Bixby call in Bixby voice setting needs to be turned on) ᄆ Model that can support long-distance voice recognition function ᄆ Remote control microphone front reference distance within 1.5M to 2M
