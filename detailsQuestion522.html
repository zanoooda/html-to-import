Noise standard Due to various circuits and large currents applied to the TV, noise may be generated as electrical components operate according to changes in screen brightness. The normal specification managed by the product is managed below 30 dB (decibel) and is similar to the noise of fans (breezes) and humidifiers. Please use it safely within the normal specification range managed by the product to the extent that it is quieter or similar to the noise generated by the humidifier.
