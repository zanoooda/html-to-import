    ※ SmartThings devices registered in the SmartThings app of mobile devices can be checked on TV. First, please register SmartThings devices in the SmartThings app of the mobile device. ■ SmartThings device connection method
    1.Select the SmartThings app on the screen of your mobile device
    ※ Please set the same AP for TV and mobile
    2. Select “+” (Add device button)
    3. Choose "Device."
    4. Enter the product name or scan the QR code of the device through "QR code scan"
    5. Start registering the device device according to the explanation on the screen of the mobile device.
    ※ In this video, SmartThings CAM was used to help you understand. 6 . Press the home button on the TV remote control to select SmartThings (digital hub) and check the connected devices on the TV screen.
