2021 Samsung TV SmartThings (Digital Hub) Connection Method (Mobile Device)

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)


  • ※ Mobile devices around the TV such as mobile phones can be automatically detected and easily connected. ■ Mobile device connection method
    1.Pull down the notification window of the mobile device from the top of the screen and press and hold the "Bluetooth" icon. * You can connect your mobile device and TV with Smart View.
    2. Select the TV model you want to connect to from the "Connectable Devices" list on your mobile device
    3. Check the PIN number in the notification window displayed on the TV screen and select the "Allow" button.
    4. The TV and mobile device are automatically connected by sound mirroring.
    5. If you want to connect to a pre-registered mobile device by sound mirroring,
    6. Press the home button on the TV remote control to select SmartThings (digital hub) → Registered mobile device selection → Sound Mirroring.
    7. Registered devices can continue to listen to the music they were listening to on their mobile devices.
    ※ This function may not be supported depending on the model.
    ※ Screen image may vary by model.
