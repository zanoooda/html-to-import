  • ※ While watching TV, it got bigger and smaller on the same channel Repeating may result in more than a broadcast signal. ■ Automatic volume control method
    1.Depending on the broadcast program produced by the broadcasting station, a voice level difference may occur, and when the channel is changed, a voice may be small or a voice may be large.
    2. It mainly occurs on wired broadcasting channels rather than regular broadcasting channels, and automatic volume function is applied to TV to compensate for different volume for each channel.
    3. When setting up the automatic volume function, the volume decreases, but all channels can be viewed at a similar volume.
    4. Press the Home or Menu button on the TV remote control to select and turn on the automatic volume in Settings → Sound → Expert Settings. ■ Settop volume control
    1.The set-top volume can be raised or lowered by pressing the +, - buttons on the set-top remote control.
    2. It is recommended to set the set-top volume to medium (50% or more) and adjust the volume of the TV.
