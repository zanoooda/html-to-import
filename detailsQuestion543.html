It's strange that there's no TV voice while watching on the set-top. (Settop, game console, etc.)


※ If there is no voice or it is strange, it may occur due to a signal abnormality in the set-top box or a cable abnormality that transmits a signal

  • ■ Sound quality test method
    1.Press the Home or Menu button on the TV remote control to set → Customer support → Device care → Self-diagnosis → Sound quality test.
    2. If the reproduced sample sound is heard neatly without noise, it is a symptom of noise due to broadcast signal problems, not product problems.
    3. In this case, you should contact the management office or broadcasters such as wired and cable IPTV to check the broadcast signal.
    4. However, if noise occurs in the reproduced sample sound, it is recommended to check the TV. ■ Settop volume control
    1.The set-top volume can be raised or lowered by pressing the +, - buttons on the set-top remote control.
    2. It is recommended to set the set-top volume to medium (50% or more) and adjust the volume of the TV. ■ Check and reconnect HDMI cable
    1.I recommend you to use a genuine cable. If the authenticated HDMI cable is not used, the screen may not be visible or a connection error may occur.
    2. Connect the set-top box and TV to check if external input connection lines such as HDMI are connected normally when watching the broadcast.
