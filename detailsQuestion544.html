When I watch it on the set-top, the TV voice is shaking, and I can hear noise in the TV voice.

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)


  • ※ 'If the voice trembles or noise occurs, it may occur when the broadcast signal is abnormal or the voice level of the input signal is not correct. ■ Sound quality test method
    1.Press the Home or Menu button on the TV remote control to set → Customer support → Device care → Self-diagnosis → Sound quality test.
    2. If the reproduced sample sound is heard neatly without noise, it is a symptom of noise due to broadcast signal problems, not product problems.
    3. In this case, you should contact broadcasters or management offices such as wired and cable IPTV to check the broadcast signal.
    4. However, if noise occurs in the reproduced sample sound, it is recommended to check the TV. ■ Settop volume control
    1.The set-top volume can be raised or lowered by pressing the +, - buttons on the set-top remote control.
    2. It is recommended to set the set-top volume to medium (50% or more) and adjust the volume of the TV. ■ Sound setting method
    1.Press the Home or Menu button on the TV remote control to select Settings → Sound → Expert Settings → Equalizer.
    2. If the sound is shaking in a specific sound field, please set the bass (100Hz, 200Hz) low in the expert setting.
