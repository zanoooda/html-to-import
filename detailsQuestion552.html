When I watch it with a TV antenna, the TV screen is blurred or the picture quality is bad.


※ If the screen is blurred on the TV, it may occur when the resolution of the broadcast signal and content is low

  • Broadcasting signal test method
    1.Press the home or menu button on the TV remote control to select Settings → Customer Support → Device Care → Self Diagnosis → Signal Information.
    2. If the antenna signal is 4 or less or the SNR value is 20 or less, it is recommended to check the broadcast signal. Signal information is a feature only available on digital broadcast channels.
    ※ If the broadcast content is not an HD signal image, the screen may be blurred or the image quality may be poor. Press the 'OK' button on the TV remote control to check if it is an HD signal image.
