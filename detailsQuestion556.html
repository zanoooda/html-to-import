The TV voice gets smaller while watching with the TV antenna, and the voice gets bigger and smaller.

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)

  • ■ Sound quality test method
    1.Press the Home or Menu button on the TV remote control to set → Customer support → Device care → Self-diagnosis → Sound quality test.
    2. If the reproduced sample sound is heard neatly without noise, it is a symptom of noise due to broadcast signal problems, not product problems.
    3. In this case, you should contact the management office or broadcasters such as wired and cable IPTV to check the broadcast signal.
    4. However, if noise occurs in the reproduced sample sound, it is recommended to check the TV. ■ Automatic volume control method
    1.Depending on the broadcast program produced by the broadcasting station, a voice level difference may occur, and when the channel is changed, a voice may be small or a voice may be large.
    2. It mainly occurs on wired broadcasting channels rather than regular broadcasting channels, and automatic volume function is applied to TV to compensate for different volume for each channel.
    3. When you set up the automatic volume function, the volume decreases, but you can watch all channels at a similar volume.
    4. Press the Home or Menu button on the TV remote control to select and turn on the automatic volume in Settings → Sound → Expert Settings.
