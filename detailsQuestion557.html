When I watch it with a TV antenna, the TV voice is shaking, and I hear noise in the TV voice.

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)

  • ■ Sound quality test method
    1.Press the Home or Menu button on the TV remote control to set → Customer support → Device care → Self-diagnosis → Sound quality test.
    2. If the reproduced sample sound is heard neatly without noise, it is a symptom of noise due to broadcast signal problems, not product problems.
    3. In this case, you should contact the management office or broadcasters such as wired and cable IPTV to check the broadcast signal.
    4. However, if noise occurs in the reproduced sample sound, it is recommended to check the TV. ■ Sound setting method
    1.Press the Home or Menu button to select Settings → Sound → Expert Settings → Equillizer.
    2. If the sound is shaking in a specific sound field, please set the bass (100 Hz, 200 Hz) low in the expert setting.
