Please tell me how to run tap view, tap sound.

1

  • Tab view, how to execute tab sound
    ①' SmartThings ' Run app - Please select 'SmartThings' app.
    ②' Menu Choose. - Please select 'Menu'.
    ③ Selection - Please select 'SmartThings Settings'.
    ④' Tap view, tap sound Choose. - Click the tab view, tab sound setting card. (Go to the function setting screen. )
    ※ Supported model - 2019 TV: The SERO - 2020 TV: UT7000 or higher / Lifestyle model, all Q series models - 2021 TV: UA7000 or higher / Lifestyle model, all Q series models
    ⑤ 'Tap view, tap sound' setting - Press the right switch icon to set 'Disable' to 'In use'.
    ※ ' Set tap view, tap sound' right away : In 'SmartThings Settings', you can set it right away by pressing the right switch icon part of the tab view, tab sound card. [ Tips. ] - When you tap, Just tap a little. - Mobile phone protection cases can be used more safely.
    2. Tab view, tab sound function not working
    ①' Top bar of screen I'm sorry. Below Low down - Please lower the 'top bar of the screen' of the mobile phone.
    ②' Battery management mode Press and hold. - Press and hold 'Battery Management Mode' to select.
    ③' Optimization mode' Selection - If the battery management mode is in power saving mode or ultra power saving mode, please change it to optimization mode.
    ※ When tap view, tap sound function is not working Is Always check the battery management mode status of your cell phone Do it, please.
