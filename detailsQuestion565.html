Please tell me how to use Digital Butler.

Function guide Source device The source device is a device that can be connected to an external port


  • 1.To use a source device, such as a game console connected by an external port, select Digital Hub from the TV menu.
    2. You can select and use the device you want from the digital hub. IR remote control device IR remote control device means home appliance using remote control.
    1.You can check the notification popup on the TV by using the remote control of the IR remote control device near the TV.
    2. Click ‘Register’ to display a control pop-up to test the IR remote control device. If you click Yes after the test, the notification popup will be displayed again.
    3. Click ‘Go to Digital Hub’ to run the digital hub.
    ※ Note. Possible devices and functions are as follows. Description of possible devices and functions Support device Support function Air-conditioner Power On / Power Off / Temperature Up / Temperature Down / Wind Up / Wind Down Air purifier Power Toggle / Wind Up / Wind Down Fan Power Toggle / Wind Up / Wind Down Robot cleaner Power Toggle/Start/Stop Humidifier Power Toggle / Wind Up / Wind Down Mobile device The method of connecting a digital hub to a mobile has to do with registering a TV in MobileSmartThings.
    1.If there is a mobile near the TV, a notification pop-up that guides the connection to the TV is displayed.
    2. Click 'Register' to display the Mobile SmartThings TV Registration Guide.
    3. When you lift the mobile and run SmartThings, the TV is detected, and when you press 'Start', the mobile-TV linkage proceeds.
    4. When interworking is completed, a notification pop-up is displayed again on the TV, and mobile can be used in the digital hub.
    5. In the digital hub, you can use the Sound Mirroring function through mobile. Some mobile devices may have different registration methods. Bluetooth device A Bluetooth device is available in a digital hub, as is the use of Bluetooth on a mobile. The Bluetooth device does not provide an alarm on the TV.
    1.You can find the Bluetooth device you want to connect to in the ‘Registerable Device’ of the Digital Hub.
    2. Click the Bluetooth device you want to connect to and click the Connect menu.
    3. You can use the Connect/Disconnect menu. The Digital Hub displays the ‘Registered Devices’ and ‘Registerable Devices’ menus. The 'registered device' is a device that can be controlled by a TV remote control and is 'registered' Go A good device' means a device that can be connected. SmartThings device SmartThings devices are Samsung's hub for controlling smart home devices and can be downloaded from both Android and iOS devices.
    1.If the TV and mobile use the same SmartThings account, the SmartThings device connected to the mobile can be used in the digital hub.
    2. Click the Bluetooth device you want to connect to and click the Connect menu.
    3. The Digital Hub provides the same features as SmartThings on TVs.
