There's static electricity, there's electrical flow.

Expected cause It may be caused by the unconnected ground outlet in the house and the antenna not being connected

  • Method of action Since home appliances operate using an AC power source of 220 V, electrical symptoms may occur due to electrical characteristics. ■ Static electricity In the case of static electricity, drying in the home can occur mainly. Please feel free to use this as it is related to the environment at home, not abnormal. ■ Electric flow generation Symptoms of electricity flow can be caused by not grounding all home appliances, including refrigerators and washing machines, and can be grounded to take action against symptoms of electricity flow. ■ How to take action ☞ Try connecting to the ground terminal of the power outlet It is recommended to connect the TV directly to the wall power outlet, not the multi-tap. ■ If there is no ground power outlet
    1.Ground by connecting the wall antenna terminal and the TV rear antenna connection terminal When using IPTV, the TV and the wall antenna may not be connected, causing symptoms.
    2. Ground by connecting the GND WIRE to the Bessa hole used for connecting the wall mount on the back of the TV Even if the above measures are taken, if the electricity flow is severe, an engineer's visit inspection is required.
