The voice recognition TV doesn't work.

Expected cause This may occur if the command is wrong or the voice recognition function is not turned on

  • Among the voice recognition functions, it may not work if the voice sensitivity is set to low or the noise or voice is small around it. The initial utterance of voice recognition is as follows - When the initial power is ON → "Power on high TV" or "Power on smart TV" - Voice recognition utterance word when watching TV → If you do "High TV" or "Smart TV", it appears at the bottom of the screen as follows (Default is HTV )
    ※ From the 2013-14 model, it is possible to command voice to turn off the power on the helpbar screen interactively in the voice recognition window. Choose to turn on the voice recognition function.
    1.Menu → Select "System"
    2. System → Select "Voice and Motion Recognition"
    3. Voice and Motion Recognition → Select "Voice Recognition"
    4. Voice recognition → Select "on"
    5. System → Voice and Motion Recognition → Select "Voice Recognition"
    6. Voice recognition → Select "voice sensitivity"
    7. Voice sensitivity → Set high in "ring"
    8. Voice sensitivity → Set "recognition sensitivity" to high If the surrounding noise is severe or the utterance (voice) is small, it may not work.
