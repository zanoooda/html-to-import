  • ■ Exterior stain checking method
    1.If a stain on the surface of the TV liquid crystal does not occur when the TV power is turned off, it may occur due to foreign substances inside the liquid crystal or abnormal broadcasting signals.
    2. In this case, you should check for abnormalities in the product through the self-diagnosis function built into the TV.
    3. If you see the same stain on the self-diagnosis screen, you need to check the product due to symptoms caused by foreign substances inside the liquid crystal.
    4. If no stains are visible on the self-diagnosis screen, they may be symptoms of screen stains caused by abnormal broadcast signals. Please check the broadcast signal through the broadcasting company you are watching.
    5. If foreign matter is seen on the exterior of the liquid crystal when the TV power is turned off, it is contaminated by foreign matter on the exterior of the liquid crystal to clean the exterior stain You can proceed. ■ Exterior stain cleaning method
    1.Soak the microfiber cloth in water, wipe it gently, and dry it with a dry cloth.
