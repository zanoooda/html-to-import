My voice is shaking.

Expected cause It may occur due to a broadcast signal abnormality or a speaker output abnormality

  • Method of action Sound quality test confirmation There may be differences in menu items and settings depending on the TV model. Please refer to the product manual. Symptoms of voice ringing or shaking can occur beyond broadcast signals or TVs, and the sound quality test function can check for abnormalities in the product. [Test Method] Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Sound Quality Test Press the remote control home button Move [Settings] at the bottom left Menu → Customer support → Move self-diagnosis Select [Start sound quality test] Confirm 20-year TV product sound quality test
    1.Press the home button of the remote control
    2. Set Please choose.
    3. Customer support → Device care Please choose.
    4. At the bottom of device care Self-diagnosis Please choose.
    5. Sound quality test Please proceed with. During the sound quality test, the sample sound built into the TV is played, and if the sample sound played is heard neatly without noise, it is a symptom of noise caused by a broadcast signal problem, not a product problem. In this case, please contact the broadcaster to check the broadcast signal [wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.]. Check the volume control method If the sound sounds strange while watching the broadcast through the set-top box, it may occur due to the difference in how to adjust the volume of the TV and the set-top box, and it can be easily adjusted. If the volume of the TV is set low (10 or less) or high, the voice level may be lowered and noise may be generated due to external influences. First, set the volume of the TV to 40 to 50, and then adjust the volume to the most suitable volume for listening with the STB remote control. [Note]
    ① When adjusting the STB volume, the volume is adjusted from 0 to 30 at the top / bottom of the TV screen.
    ② When adjusting the TV volume, the volume control is displayed from 0 to 100 on the left / right side of the TV screen. Equalizer adjustment method Among the voice signals, low-band sound is emphasized, which may cause symptoms such as voice tremors / noise. Please adjust the sound level of the low band [100Hz, 200Hz] to low on the TV.
