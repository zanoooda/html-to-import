Function guide Connecting TV to SmartThings SmartThings It is a new name for Samsung Connect

  • It is a function that conveniently controls and manages smart home appliances and Internet of Things (IoT) devices with smartphones. It is an open source platform that can connect not only our company but also other IoT products, and is a next-generation platform service that will integrate Samsung's products into one. Target product 2017 TV → FHD: M5500 or higher, UHD: MU6190 or higher, QLED: 7/8/9 series 2018 TV → FHD: N4000 or higher, UHD: NU7400 or higher, QLED: 7/8/9 series 2019 TV → UHD: RU6990 or higher, QLED: 6/7/8/9 series Registering TV in SmartThings
    ① Run SmartThings application
    ② Connectable devices are automatically searched
    ③ Select Add Now in Pop-up Window
    ④ Next selection on the TV Start screen
    ⑤ Select the place where the TV is installed and then
    ⑥ TV connection in progress In the case of TV in 2016, After checking the pop-up window displayed at the top of the TV screen, select Allow using the TV remote control
    ⑦ Enter the PIN displayed on the TV in the SmartThings app of the mobile phone and select completion
    ※ Allowable selection is possible only with the TV remote control, so please prepare the TV remote control. For TVs after 2017, Enter the PIN displayed on the TV screen into the mobile phone SmartThings and select Complete.
