Please let me know how to test image quality among TV self-diagnosis functions in 2017 and 2018.

Setting method - Press the home button of the remote control → Settings → Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Image quality test [Press remote control home button] [Move left lower setting] [Menu → Move Customer Support ] [Self-diagnosis Selection] [Select to start the image quality test] [Sample image stored on TV is displayed] [If there are no problem symptoms, select no] [Select to close image quality test completion]
※ Sample images are played on the screen during image quality tests, and if the image is clearly visible, image quality abnormalities may occur due to abnormalities in external broadcast signals, not product abnormalities

  • In this case, you should contact the cable broadcaster you are watching for a signal check. If the sample image is not clear during the image quality test, it may occur due to internal symptoms of LCD panel or TV. Please get an accurate diagnosis from a professional engineer for that part.
