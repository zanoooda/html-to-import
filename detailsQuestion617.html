Expected cause It can occur when watching analog (ATV) broadcasts or when broadcasting signals are unstable

TV can watch digital broadcasting and analog (wired) broadcasting at the same time, which can occur if the broadcast signal is weak when watching analog wired broadcasting channels. In this case, please contact the broadcaster to check the broadcast signal [wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.]. Self-diagnosis signal information checking method Symptoms that overlap as if a shadow appears on the screen on a specific channel can occur when the broadcast signal is weak, In this case, you can check the abnormality of the product through the function of checking the sensitivity of the broadcast signal built into the TV. ■ Setting method Press the home button of the remote control → Settings → Menu → Customer Support → Self-diagnosis → Start signal information Customer Support - Self Diagnosis - Start Signal Information Check the signal information for the channel while watching. If there are less than 4 antenna signals or the SNR value is less than 20, it is recommended to check the broadcast signal in this case if the broadcast signal is weak.
