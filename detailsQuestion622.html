  • 1.Symptoms of TV sound not coming out or disconnection on the sound bar are often caused by loose connection terminals or poor contact with outlets.
    2. If it is connected by HDMI, please completely separate and reconnect the power cord of the one-connect and sound bar.
    3. Separate the HDMI cable and connect it to the HDMI input (ARC) terminal of the one-connect and the HDMI OUT (TV-ARC) terminal of the sound bar.
    4. If connected by BT (wireless), please completely separate and reconnect the power cord of the TV and sound bar.
    5. Press the external input button on the side button of the sound bar to switch to BT mode, and press it for another 5 seconds to select the sound bar from the TV's speaker list in pairing state.
    6. The speaker list of the TV is connected by pressing the home or menu button of the remote control to select the sound bar model name from the setting → sound → sound output → speaker list.
