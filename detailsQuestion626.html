QLED TV, screen is blurry

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)


  • 1.If the screen is blurred on the TV, it may occur when the resolution of the broadcast signal, content, or set-top box is low.
    2. First of all, if you're watching on the airwaves through the antenna,
    3. Please reconnect the antenna cable.
    4. Please change the channel to the TV remote control and check if the channel notation is displayed as 9-1, 10-1, 11-1 on the screen. If it is changed to 9, 10, 11 without the "-" mark, it is an analog broadcast, not a digital broadcast.
    5. In this case, manually press any digital channel such as 9-1 or 10-1,11-1 to check.
    6. If the digital channel is not selected or outputted, please reset the TV automatic channel. (Home → Settings → Channel → Enter the automatic channel and search for the channel. The number of channels searched may vary depending on the region, broadcaster, and surrounding environment. )
    7. If you're using a set-top box,
    8. Please reconnect HDMI or component cable.
    9. Please contact the set-top and carrier to set the set-top box resolution to suit the TV. (FHD model 1080P, UHD model 2160P)
