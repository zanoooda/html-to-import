QLED TV, no network, intermittent disconnection.

To watch the video, press the Play button (▶)


  • 1.Network failure or disconnection on the TV usually occurs depending on the router and network status.
    2. Please disconnect and reconnect the power cord of the Internet router completely.
    3. Please completely disconnect and reconnect the LAN wire connected to the Internet router. The mode LED on the Internet router flashes, which is normal.
    4. It may take 1~2 minutes to stabilize the Internet router.
    5. Please reset the network of the TV. Enter home and proceed with network initialization by entering Settings → General → Network.
    6. Please reset the wireless network when the TV is not connected to the wireless network. Home → Settings → General → Network → Wireless → Select router → Reset by entering password. To watch the video, press the Play button (▶).
