I can't recognize the new device.

Expected cause It may be caused by high current used in USB devices

  • Method of action Check USB extension cable removal When connecting a USB external HDD to a TV, you must connect it to a USB (HDD) terminal. When connecting to a terminal other than the USB (HDD) terminal, a USB power excess message may occur. USB terminal basically supports 5 V 0.5 A, which is USB
    2.0 standard, and USB (HDD) to cope with USB external HDD We are applying 5 V 1 A spec. When using USB external HDD, please connect to USB (HDD) terminal and use it. If you use Y-shaped auxiliary power, you can use it after connecting to USB (HDD) terminal and other USB terminal on the side of the TV.
