I can't change the channel.

Expected cause It may occur due to set-top box malfunction or TV malfunction

  • Method of action Check the set-top box. This is because when watching TV broadcasts by connecting the set-top box, the TV acts as a monitor and changes to the channel you want to watch in the set-top box. If the channel is not changed, it may occur due to set-top box malfunction. Remove the power cord of the set-top box for about a minute and reconnect it, and in the same case, contact the broadcaster (cable / sky life / IPTV) for an inspection of the set-top box. Change remote control mode settings If the remote control mode is selected as a product other than the TV, the remote control may not work. In this case, please select the device selection toward the TV and use it. When you press the remote control mode button, it changes in the order of TV → STB → AC. If you have the same symptoms after checking the remote control mode selection, please proceed with the TV initialization method.
