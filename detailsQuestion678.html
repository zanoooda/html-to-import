I want to know the cell phone that can't be recognized on TV.

Expected cause The connection method may vary depending on the smartphone connection method

  • Method of action Check the cell phone that is not recognized on the TV When connecting a smartphone and a PC, there are removable disk methods and MTP methods. In both ways, data from mobile phones can be backed up to PCs, and only smartphones that support removable disk methods can be connected when connected to TVs. The MTP method is not recognized on TV. MTP is an abbreviation of the Media Transfer Protocol, which refers to a multimedia media-related protocol method.
