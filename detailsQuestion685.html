The melody is loud when the TV is on and off.

Expected cause It may occur because the melody sound is loud

  • Method of action Melody release method You can choose between turning off, small, medium, and large melody functions. If the melody is loud, try changing the settings to medium or small. (Support functions vary depending on TV model ) Select Menu → Settings (Initial Settings) → Melody → Off on the remote control. Menu - Settings (Initial Settings) Melody - Select Off The method of entering the melody setting varies depending on the year-by-year model. Please refer to the user manual for detailed setting methods.
