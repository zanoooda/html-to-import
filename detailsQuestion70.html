It stops while recording a TV broadcast.

Expected cause It may be caused by low performance of external storage (memory, HDD)

  • Method of action The recording function is a function using an external device and is a problem caused by the performance of the storage device. The device's performance is low and cannot be recorded. It is recommended to use other storage devices when a warning window called ' occurs. The performance constraints of the recording function (PVR) are as follows. Memory: Free space of 4GB or more, read/write speed of 20/34mps or more, respectively) - Card reader is not recommended. HDD : A rotation speed of
    2.5 inches or more at 5,400 rpm - 1.8/3.5 inches is not recommended due to unstable power and voltage.
