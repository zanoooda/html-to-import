Heat generation by product Product name Details LED - In the case of edge LEDs, LED BARs are located in certain areas of the top, bottom, left, and right due to their characteristics, so only certain areas can be hotter, and the product is slim and has high thermal conductivity, so it can feel hotter than other product groups. - You can see that the LED BAR, which is a light source, is installed in the hottest part by touching it with your hand. LCD - Back light is lamp, so heat is generated overall, and heat is generated most at the top of the front.
