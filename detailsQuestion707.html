Even if I turn on the TV, I can see the light at the bottom.

Expected cause Light effects can occur while watching or when set to Always

  • [Light on Samsung logo at the bottom of TV] Method of action Light effect setting method The light effect function that lights up the Samsung logo at the bottom of the TV is set, so you can see the light on the Samsung logo while watching TV. If you see a light on the Samsung logo while watching, please change the light effect [off, on standby].
    ① Turn off - Samsung logo always lights off.
    ② Standby - Samsung logo lights up when power is off.
    ③ Watching - Samsung logo lights up when power is on.
    ④ Always - Samsung logo always lights up. [Menu - System] [General Settings] [Turn off light effect, select standby]
