Each channel has a different voice.

Expected cause The automatic volume setting may be changed, resulting in a change in voice size while watching

  • Method of action Automatic volume control method - There may be a difference in voice level depending on the broadcast program produced by the broadcasting station, and when the channel is changed, the voice may be small or the voice may be large. - It occurs mainly in wired broadcasting channels rather than regular broadcasting channels, and automatic volume function is applied to TV to compensate for different volume for each channel. - When setting the automatic volume function, the volume decreases, but all channels can be viewed at a similar volume. [Menu → Volume → Speaker Settings → Automatic Volume → Normal [Late Night] Selection]
    ※ Note - Automatic volume adjustment function by year Guidelines for Automatic Volume Adjustment Functions by Year Year Entry method 2009 model year Menu → Volume → Automatic Volume → On 2010 model Menu → Volume → Automatic Volume → Normal (late night) Models after 2011 Menu → Volume → Speaker Settings → Automatic Volume → Normal (late night)
