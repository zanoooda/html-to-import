I can't get a voice when I connect to the PC.

Expected cause It can be caused by wrong cable connection, mute, and voice output settings

  • Method of action Cable reconnection ■ When connected by stereo cable Please reconnect the voice input of the TV and the voice OUT terminal of the PC (notebook). ■ When connected by HDMI-MDMI cable Please reconnect the voice input of the TV and the voice OUT terminal of the PC (notebook). PC voice setting and TV volume adjustment Press the speaker at the bottom right of the PC and check the mute and adjust the volume in the volume mixer. Voice output change Select sound from the control panel Right mouse selection in HDM I Out Select Settings for Use and Default Devices Complete screen with HDMI
