This is the content of beep (whistle) noise in PC mode

  • Target model - PDP PN51D580A1G and all other models cause - Distributor characteristic failure (USB power method) The power method of the distributor is to receive 5V from the USB terminal of the PC It may be caused by a significant difference between the PC USB power supply end and the output end of the distributor. How to check and take action - Use a separate USB adapter at the PC terminal to supply USB distributor power Connect the power outlet. - Or, it is recommended to use a distributor in 220V adapter format.
