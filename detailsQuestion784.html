I wonder how to take action when I feel static electricity (electric flow) on the TV.

Method of action Since home appliances operate using 220V of AC power, electrical symptoms may occur due to electrical characteristics

  • static electricity In the case of static electricity, drying in the home can occur mainly. Please feel free to use this as it is related to the environment at home. Electricity flow and leakage current generation Symptoms of electricity flow can be caused by not grounding all home appliances, including refrigerators and washing machines, and can be grounded to take action against symptoms of electricity flow. Method of action When connected to the ground terminal of the power outlet, it can be used without leakage current. It is recommended to connect the TV directly to the wall power outlet, not the multi-tap. If there is no ground power outlet
    1.When using ground IPTV by connecting the wall antenna terminal and the TV rear antenna connection terminal, the TV and the wall antenna are not connected, which may cause symptoms.
    2. Please connect the GND WIRE to the Bessa Hall used for connecting the wall mount on the back of the TV and ground it.
