I can't connect my 3D glasses.

Motion distance guide Please keep the proper distance for pairing and watching TV when using 3D glasses

  • Pairing or 3D TV viewing may not be possible. - Appropriate distance for pairing: Within 50 cm - Appropriate distance when watching TV: 2 ~ 6 m Battery replacement/charging method If the red LED above the glasses flashes every 2 seconds or the LED light does not work at all even if you press the button, the battery is discharged, so replace or charge the battery. SSG-4100GB (battery type) battery replacement method Insert the battery in the same direction as the + indicated on the battery cover. SSG-3550CR (Rechargeable) Charging Method
    ① Charging: Orange LEDs blink every second.
    ② Charging complete: Green LEDs blink every 2 seconds.
    ③ It takes about 30 minutes to fully charge after discharge. Pairing reset method The pairing method is as follows.
    ① First, turn on the TV.
    ② When you press the power button of 3D glasses for 2 to 3 seconds, the green and red LEDs blink alternately and turn off, saying, "3D glasses are connected to the TV screen If you get the message ", it's done. 3D setting method Press the "3D" shortcut on the TV remote to switch to 3D. Press the 3D shortcut key Completed when the stereoscopic image screen appears
