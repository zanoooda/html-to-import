Broadcast signal check confirmation guide If a horizontal line occurs only on a specific channel, it may occur due to a broadcast signal abnormality, not a product abnormality

  • If there is an abnormality in the product, a line is formed in the same position on all channels, but if a line is generated by a broadcast signal, the position where the line is formed varies from channel to channel. In this case, please contact the broadcaster (wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.) to check the broadcast signal. Broadcast signal check confirmation guide When you press the menu button on the remote control, you can check for product abnormalities using the menu window displayed. ☞ If the line appears to be covered by the menu window, it is necessary to check the broadcast signal as a symptom of a line occurring due to a broadcast signal abnormality. In this case, please contact the broadcaster (wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.) to check the broadcast signal. ☞ If the line appears on the menu window as it is, it is a symptom of product abnormality and needs to be checked by the engineer. Self-diagnosis method The cause of the line on the screen may be a broadcast signal problem, a TV abnormality, or an abnormality in the product can be checked through the image quality test function. How to check image quality test Select [Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Image Quality Test]. Menu - Customer Support Self-diagnosis Image quality test Checking for abnormality During the image quality test, the high-definition image built into the TV is displayed on the screen, and if the high-definition image does not have a line and is clearly visible, , it is a symptom caused by a broadcast signal problem, not a product problem. In this case, please contact the broadcaster to check the broadcast signal [wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.]. If there is a line in the high-definition image displayed, it can cause circuit abnormalities and you need to check it out.
