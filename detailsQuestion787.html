Broadcasting signal check guide Symptoms that occur on a specific channel may occur due to a broadcast signal abnormality, not a product abnormality

  • TVs can watch digital and analog broadcasts at the same time, which can occur if the broadcast signal is weak when watching analog wired broadcasting channels. In this case, please contact the broadcaster for a broadcast signal check [wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.]. Self-diagnosis method If the image quality looks bad, it may occur due to a broadcast signal problem, TV abnormality, or abnormality of the product can be checked through the image quality test function. Image quality test entry method Run [Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Image Quality Test]. Menu - Customer Support Self-diagnosis Image quality test Checking for abnormality During the image quality test, the high-definition image built into the TV is displayed on the screen, and if the high-definition image is clearly visible without noise , it is a symptom caused by a broadcast signal problem, not a product problem. In this case, please contact the broadcaster (wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.) to check the broadcast signal. If there is noise in the displayed high-definition image, it may occur due to circuit abnormalities and you need to check it. Check the connection between the set-top box and the TV You need to check the connection terminal to connect the set-top box and watch the broadcast in high definition. If you watch a broadcast with an external input, image quality deterioration may occur, and you need to change the connection between the TV and the set-top box with the component/HDMI terminal to watch a high-definition broadcast. Set-top box resolution change If the resolution of the set-top box is set low, image quality deterioration may occur. [How to check set-top box resolution] Press the Information Display button on the TV remote control and check the resolution displayed at the top of the TV screen. If the displayed resolution is set to 720*480, it needs to be changed to 1920*1080. [How to change the resolution of set-top box] Depending on the set-top box model, there is a button or menu setting method for selecting resolution. In the case of button type, you can change it by pressing the set-top body resolution button, and in the case of changing it in the menu, please contact the broadcaster. Even if the resolution is adjusted, if the same symptom occurs, it is necessary to reconnect the HDMI cable connected to the set-top and TV and reboot the set-top box. Matters to be verified; TV and set-top power OFF TV back cable Reconnect after separation (HDMI, component) If it is difficult to separate from the TV, Reconnect after disconnecting cable (HDMI, component) Main switch on the back of the set-top box ON 1 minute after OFF Set-top booting takes at least 1 to 10 minutes It's done when the broadcast comes out.
