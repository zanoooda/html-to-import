Broadcasting signal check guide If a screen break occurs on a specific broadcasting channel, it may be caused by a weak reception of a broadcast signal, not a product abnormality

  • Unlike analog TVs used in the past, digital TVs appear to be abnormal in products due to symptoms such as screen stop and screen breakage when broadcasting signals are weak, but you should contact broadcasters (wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.) to check broadcasting signals. Signal strength checking method Symptoms of screen breaking while watching can occur due to broadcast signal problems, TV abnormalities, and abnormality of the product can be checked through the digital signal strength function. [Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Digital Signal Intensity] By executing the digital signal strength, the antenna must receive at least 3 spaces to watch normal digital broadcasting, and if the antenna reception is displayed below 3 spaces, screen breakage symptoms may occur while watching the broadcast. In this case, please contact the broadcaster (wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.) to check the broadcast signal.
