Only certain channels have poor image quality.

Expected cause It can occur when watching analog broadcast channels

  • It can occur depending on the broadcaster's content (airwave broadcasting, local cable broadcasting). Method of action If the image quality of only a specific channel is poor, it may occur due to poor image quality or poor reception of broadcast signals in the process of producing a broadcast program. In this case, please contact the broadcaster to check the broadcast signal [wired, cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.]. If the image quality of only a specific channel is poor, it may occur due to a broadcast signal problem, may occur due to TV abnormalities, and the image quality test function can check whether the product is abnormal or not. Self-diagnosis image quality test confirmation [Menu → Customer Support → Self Diagnosis → Image Quality Test] [Click remote control home button] [Move left lower setting] [Menu → Move Customer Support] [Self-diagnosis Selection] [Select to start image quality test] [Sample images stored on TV are displayed] When conducting the image quality test, the high-definition image built into the TV is displayed on the screen, and if the image is clearly visible without noise , it is a symptom caused by a broadcast signal problem. In this case, you should contact the broadcaster for a broadcast signal check [cable, skylife, IPTV, etc.]
